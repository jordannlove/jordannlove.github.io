About Me

I’ve managed to accrue a balance of design and technical experience; I’ve been involved in design work since my early high school days. I recently completed contract work with a health technology startup where I redesigned the user interface of their product, working with them to create a new brand and style guide. The previous summer, I did a remote research experience for undergraduates (REU) at Carnegie Mellon University, where I created a prototype for an educational dashboard and conducted user interviews with middle school students. I’ve done other design work through my classes and through freelancing.

On the technical side, I’ve been able to hone my web development skills through hackathon projects, front-end development internships, and coursework such as Mobile Development, Software Engineering, and Introduction to Machine Learning.

At KU, I just completed my term as President of the Women in Computing organization and also just graduated from the Self Engineering Leadership Fellowship. Outside of school, you can find me hiking, trying out local coffee shops, or (poorly) roller skating!

Design Samples

Here are some examples of my work, ranging between graphic elements and prototypes.

1SRCE User Interface

I worked closely with a healthcare technology startup called 1SRCE to create a new user interface for their product. I created detailed mockups in Figma, highlighting interactions while also determining the color scheme, iconography, and typography for the product, while also working within Material Design guidelines. Additionally, I compiled this information into a style guide to allow for easy handoff to the developers.

Happy Earth HCI Project

I used the human-centered design process to create wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes, and usability tests for a recycling app intended for an emerging market (a “Next Billion Users” population).

Carnegie Mellon Lynnette Dashboard

I researched middle school users of Lynnette algebra tutoring software by conducting user interviews and tests of a Marvel prototype. I also created a high-fidelity Figma prototype of the dashboard.
